Our Story

With over 12 years of industry expertise, Erbil Jewellery LLC has established itself as a globally trusted name in the jewellery world. Since beginning operations in 2013, we have earned a reputation for delivering jewellery of exceptional quality, combining global vision with traditional craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to detail and value. Our collections are meticulously designed and crafted by experienced professionals who bring passion and precision to every piece. From intricate detailing to consistent quality, Erbil Jewellery stands as a hallmark of trust and reliability for our customers, partners, and stakeholders. Our strong presence spans across 30+ countries and 6 continents, serving a distinguished global clientele. This global reach is a testament to our consistent adherence to stringent quality controls and timely delivery schedules. At Erbil Jewellery, customer satisfaction is our highest priority.
